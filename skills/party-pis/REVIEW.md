# Ethics Review: National Conservatives (NC)

**Profile file:** skills/party-nc/SKILL.md
**Review date:** 2026-05-27
**Process:** LLM-pair review (progressive + conservative analyst personas)

## Reviewer A (Progressive Analyst)

The NC profile presents national-conservative positions accurately and without caricature. The social transfer achievements (800+ program, poverty reduction) are acknowledged factually, which prevents the profile from being a simple "villain" characterization. The social conservatism is grounded in Catholic social teaching as the party frames it, rather than in hostility toward specific groups.

**Issues identified:**

1. **"Liberal elites" rhetoric framing**: The rhetorical style section accurately describes NC's use of "liberal elites" as a framing device, but an AI agent using this language in debate could produce content that reads as endorsing a particular political antagonism. Recommend noting in the rhetorical style section that this is a documented framing device of the party, and that the agent should use it descriptively (as the party frames it) not prescriptively.

2. **LGBTQ+ section**: The red lines state NC "never supports constitutional recognition of same-sex marriage" — this is factually accurate. It does not use dehumanizing language and correctly grounds the position in the party's value framework rather than in anti-LGBTQ+ sentiment per se. No change needed.

**Harmful output risk:** Low. The profile accurately presents a governing-capable political party's positions without enabling hate speech. The Output Constraints section prevents generation of slurs or targeted content.

## Reviewer B (Conservative Analyst)

The NC profile accurately represents a major European governing party that received approximately 35% of the vote in its last election — it is not a fringe position. The sovereignty argument regarding judicial organization is presented as the party frames it, not as a strawman. The social transfer achievements are correctly highlighted as genuine policy accomplishments.

**Issues identified:**

1. **Immigration section nuance**: The description of "cultural neighbors" (Ukraine, Belarus) as preferred immigration sources could generate content that appears to endorse ethnic/national preference in immigration. Should add that this reflects the party's stated policy of culturally managed migration, not ethnic hierarchy, and that the party has a legal pathway for all nationalities.

2. **Historical references**: The rhetorical style section mentions "references to historical injustice (partitions, WWII)" — this is accurate but an agent should contextualize these as rhetorical appeals, not as implying current grievances against neighboring countries.

**Harmful output risk:** Low. The profile is accurate and policy-grounded. No content that could generate dehumanizing outputs detected.

## Corrections Applied

| Issue | Location in SKILL.md | Correction |
|-------|----------------------|------------|
| "Liberal elites" framing note | Rhetorical Style section | Added parenthetical note that "liberal elites" is a documented party rhetorical device to frame debates, not a factual descriptor of opponents |

## Verdict

APPROVED WITH CORRECTIONS
