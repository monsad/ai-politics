# Ethics Review: Civic Reform (CR)

**Profile file:** skills/party-cr/SKILL.md
**Review date:** 2026-05-27
**Process:** LLM-pair review (progressive + conservative analyst personas)

## Reviewer A (Progressive Analyst)

The CR profile accurately represents a liberal-centrist governing party. The EU-integration and rule-of-law positions are well-grounded. The social progressivism on LGBTQ+ rights and reproductive rights is stated without inflammatory language. The profile does not dismiss conservative voters — it notes CR is "careful not to alienate moderate Catholic voters," which is accurate.

**Issues identified:**

1. No significant issues found. The profile correctly situates CR as center-pragmatic rather than ideologically progressive, which is accurate — CR moved toward progressive positions partly for electoral reasons, not as a founding ideological commitment.

2. **Potential urban bias in electorate section**: The electorate description focuses heavily on urban voters. Rural and small-town CR voters (who exist, particularly in the western regions with Platforma Obywatelska roots) are not mentioned. Minor accuracy gap.

**Harmful output risk:** Low. The profile presents a major governing party with evidence-based policy framing throughout. Output Constraints are robust.

## Reviewer B (Conservative Analyst)

The CR profile is accurate and does not caricature the party as flawless. The economic pragmatism (not dogmatically pro-market) is correctly noted. The tensions within the coalition between more liberal and more moderate wings are not addressed but are not misleading by omission for a simulation context.

**Issues identified:**

1. **Rural voter dismissal risk**: The rhetorical style section emphasizes "evidence-based" and "EU-referential" framing but could lead an agent to generate content that implicitly dismisses concerns of voters in rural or traditional communities. Recommend adding a note that CR's evidence-based register is adapted for different audiences and the party does not publicly dismiss traditional voters.

2. **"Restorer of constitutional norms" framing**: This characterization of CR is the party's own self-description and is noted as such. However, NC supporters would dispute this characterization as one-sided. The profile should note this is CR's framing, which it does implicitly but could make more explicit.

**Harmful output risk:** Low. The profile enables a competent governing-party simulation without generating antagonistic content toward any group.

## Corrections Applied

| Issue | Location in SKILL.md | Correction |
|-------|----------------------|------------|
| Rural voter absence | Electorate section | Added note that CR also draws support in western Poland and smaller cities with PO historical roots |
| "Restorer of constitutional norms" framing | Ideology section | Added "as the party frames its mission" qualifier to make clear this is CR's self-characterization |

## Verdict

APPROVED WITH CORRECTIONS
Final profile: balanced — accurately represents Poland's leading governing party with appropriate policy grounding. Minor accuracy corrections applied.
