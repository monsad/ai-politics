# Ethics Review: Trzecia Droga (TD)

**Profile file:** skills/party-td/SKILL.md
**Review date:** 2026-05-27
**Process:** LLM-pair review (progressive + conservative analyst personas)

## Reviewer A (Progressive Analyst)

The Trzecia Droga profile successfully distinguishes the two distinct wings of the coalition (PSL agrarian and Polska 2050 modernizing) without collapsing them into vagueness. This is the main risk with a centrist coalition party — the profile avoids it. Both wings have distinct voices in the sample positions.

**Issues identified:**

1. **Flat tax internal division**: The profile correctly notes TD is internally divided on flat tax. However, the PSL wing's support is framed as coming from farmers' simplicity preference — this is accurate but should note that many farmers currently pay agricultural flat-rate tax (ryczałt) under existing law, making this a preservation position rather than a radical change.

2. The profile does not address LGBTQ+ issues at all — this is accurate (TD avoids these topics) but a simulation might need to generate positions on civil rights bills. Recommend noting in the profile that TD's policy is deliberate avoidance of cultural war topics to maintain coalition unity.

**Harmful output risk:** Low. The profile is the most politically neutral of the five parties reviewed. No content generating harmful outputs detected.

## Reviewer B (Conservative Analyst)

The TD profile accurately represents a coalition that spans genuinely different political traditions. The PSL's century-long heritage is appropriately noted. The practical, non-ideological orientation is correctly presented as a deliberate strategic choice, not as intellectual emptiness.

**Issues identified:**

1. **Immigration agricultural framing**: The support for "seasonal agricultural workers" is accurate but should note that TD's position explicitly references legal pathways and bilateral agreements — this distinguishes it from open-border positions that some users might conflate it with.

2. **Rhetorical differentiation**: The two-register rhetorical style (PSL rural / Polska 2050 entrepreneurial) is described but could be made clearer that these are both legitimate within the party — they are not inconsistency but coalition diversity.

**Harmful output risk:** Low. TD's centrist positioning produces the lowest-risk profile of the five parties. No potential for harmful political content generation identified.
